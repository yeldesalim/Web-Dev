Recipe #1:New York baked cheesecake

  • Time: 1h
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
Packet Plain sweet biscuits 250 g
Melted butter 125 g
Cream cheese, softened 2 * 250g packets
Caster sugar 3/4 cup
Bulla Light Sour Cream 3/4 cup
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Finely grated lemon rind 1 tsp
Eggs 3
Frozen raspberries, thawed, to serve

Step 1: Preheat oven to 160°C/140°C fan-forced. Grease a 6cm-deep, 22cm round (base) spring-form cake pan.

Step 2:Process biscuits until mixture resembles fine breadcrumbs. Add butter. Process until combined. Press mixture over base and sides of pan leaving a 2cm gap from top (see Cooking class). Refrigerate for 30 minutes.

Step 3:Process cream cheese, sugar, sour cream, vanilla and lemon rind until smooth. Add eggs, 1 at a time, processing until just combined. Pour into prepared pan. Bake for 50 minutes to 1 hour or until just set and centre wobbles slightly. Allow to cool in oven for 2 hours, with door ajar. Refrigerate overnight. Serve with raspberries.

Recipe #2:Chicken teriyaki pineapple rice

  • Time: 25min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
soy sauce 80ml (1/3 cup)
mirin seasoning 80ml (1/3 cup)
cooking sake 80ml (1/3 cup)
pineapple pieces in juice, drained, juice reserved 225g can
garlic cloves, crushed 2
finely grated ginger 2 tsp
caster sugar 2 tbs
vegetable oil 1 tbs
chicken breasts or thigh fillets, cut into 2-3cm pieces 600 g
cornflour 2 tsp
sesame oil 1 tsp
Steamed rice, to serve
Sliced green shallots, to serve

Step 1:Combine the soy sauce, mirin, sake, pineapple juice, garlic, ginger and sugar in a jug.

Step 2:Heat the oil in a large, deep frying pan or wok over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken, in 3 batches, until deep golden brown.

Step 3:Reduce the heat to medium and add the sauce mixture to the pan. Return all the chicken to the pan, cover and bring to a simmer. Tilt the lid slightly and simmer for 10 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.

Step 4:Combine the cornflour with 2 tbs water in a small bowl. Add to the pan along with the pineapple pieces and sesame oil. Cook, stirring, for 1-2 minutes or until mixture bubbles and thickens slightly.

Step 5:Serve over steamed rice, sprinkled with green shallots.

Source: Chicken teriyaki pineapple rice recipe website

Recipe #3:Summer party punch

  • Time: 05min
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
water 1/3 cup
pineapple juice 2 cups
chilled sparkling mineral water 1cup
raspberries 125g
blueberries 125g
mango, peeled, sliced 1
fresh mint sprigs 1/2 cup
seedless red grapes, halved 100 g
ice cubes, to serve
Lemon slices, to serve

Step 1:Place star anise, 1/3 cup sugar and 1/3 cup water in a small saucepan. Stir over medium heat for 2 minutes or until sugar dissolves. Remove from heat. Place in a large bowl. Stir in pineapple juice and mineral water.

Step 2:Place fruit, half the mint and ice in a large jug. Top with juice mixture. Serve punch with lemon slices and top with remaining mint.
